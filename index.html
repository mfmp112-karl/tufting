Black & White Rugs

Turn your first 3 tufted rugs into a TikTok page and your first orders. Brand, plan, and 7 copy-ready posts.

The Brand Angle

All three of your rugs are black and white. That's not a limitation - it's your identity. Build "the black-and-white rug account."

  • Instant cohesion. Your grid looks like a brand from day one, even with only a few posts.
  • Scroll-stopping. A monochrome feed stands out in a niche drowning in rainbow yarn.
  • Premium feel. B&W reads as "art," which supports higher custom pricing.

Keep every thumbnail, backdrop, and prop in the same monochrome world so the page stays unmistakably yours.

Content Multiplication

You don't need 3 videos - you need ~15. Each finished rug is a content mine:

  • Styled reveal - rug on a clean wall/floor, good light, slow pan, trending sound.
  • Texture / ASMR close-up - macro of the pile, hand running over the carved edges.
  • "Rate my first 3 rugs 1-10" - comment-bait the algorithm loves.
  • "Which should I sell first?" - everyone who votes is a warm lead.
  • Custom pitch - hold up the text rug: "this can say literally anything."
  • Then start filming your next rug from line one - the start-to-finish build video is the evergreen engine that never runs out.

Bio

🖤 Black & white handmade rugs
✦ Custom logos, portraits & text

👇 Shop / order form

Pin your strongest reveal (probably Heisenberg) to the top of your profile.

The non-negotiables

  • A CTA on every single post. "DM to order," "link in bio," "comment your design." Never post a rug without telling people how to buy.
  • Sell capability, not inventory. You don't need a shelf of stock - the text rug proves typography, Heisenberg proves portraits. Open for commissions now.
  • The first-order hack. Gift one free small logo rug to a local business or mid-size creator. One share from them can land your first handful of paying orders.
  • Reply to every comment & DM fast. Early engagement and quick replies are what convert curiosity into a sale.
